# Meet the sidebar

Click the **Mneme** icon on the Activity Bar (the vertical strip on the far left).

You'll see six stacked panels:

1. **God Nodes** — the most-connected concepts in your workspace. Your "boss fights".
2. **Drift Findings** — live results from the auditors, grouped by severity.
3. **Step Ledger** — numbered plan of work, status-tracked.
4. **Recent Decisions** — the last 20 entries from your decision ledger.
5. **Recent Queries** — the last 10 recall/blast queries you ran.
6. **Project Shards** — every project mneme has indexed on this machine.

Every node is clickable. Hover for a tooltip; right-click for actions.
